Why ePublish?

Everyone says, you get what you pay for. But what if you actually got more… a lot more? ePublish, the new digital publishing workflow solution exclusive to Rhapsody, gives you more, saving you time and streamlining the entire publishing workflow.

ePublish is a revolutionary CMS based, digital publishing platform perfectly designed for non-technical production and design staff. Now you can produce dynamic, HTML5 digital apps and responsive mobile websites directly from Adobe InDesign files, and automate the entire publishing process, from design to deployment across multiple devices. Epublish is developed by Rhapsody, the leading cross-media production specialist providing digital and technical solutions for many of the UK’s largest publishers and retailers.

Features and Functionalty

One publication, any device, using our fully responsive design framework

Speed up your digital publishing workflow with our automated InDesign plugin

Open up the digital publishing process to non technical staff

Create iOS, Android, Windows 8, Adobe DPS apps or mobile optimized websites

Integrate with a range of 3rd party app containers such as Adobe DPS or PugPig

Integrate with a range of familiar analytics packages such as Google Analytics and Omniture
